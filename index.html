The beginnings of a pandemic

Covid-19 in Scotland - the first 100 days of the pandemic, analysed.

A chart following key events and growth in Covid deaths in Scotland at the beginning of the pandemic

  • The line and area beneath the graph represent cumulative deaths in Scotland and show the growth of deaths following confirmation of the first Covid case in Scotland on March 1st 2020. Hover over the line to read the cumulative death figure on a given day.
  • Red circles show notable events as they unfolded in Scotland. Hover over the circles for more information and click on links to news stories.

This chart was built using d3js. Covid deaths data is from the Scottish Government and analysis of news coverage over the time period from BBC News Scotland and various news sources.
